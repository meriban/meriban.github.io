These were written as accompanying material for the [MDG Library Carpentry workshop 2026 in Birmingham](/workshops/cilip_mdg_lc_bham_2026_05/) and are meant as foundational material for working with non-MARC data. Though they tackle quite technical topics and concepts, they are written for (metadata) librarians without a techincal background and thus aim to be very accessible and in plain English. However, they build on each other and an exercise is running through them (except for [Data types](/foundations/data_types/) and [Data encoding](/foundations/encoding/)). Further material used in the workshop can be found on the workshop site: [MDG Library Carpentry workshop 2026 in Birmingham](/workshops/cilip_mdg_lc_bham_2026_05/).
